# 3-Month Social Media Content Calendar
## Cultivated Growth Network

**Goal:** Drive traffic to book large corporate clients over 3-month period
**Platforms:** LinkedIn (primary) & Instagram (secondary)
**Target Audience:** Sales professionals, sales managers, business owners, corporate decision-makers
**Key Metrics:** Engagement rate, saves, profile visits, DM inquiries, website clicks

---

## Content Mix Strategy

### Weekly Posting Cadence:
- **LinkedIn:** 5-7 posts per week
- **Instagram:** 5-7 posts per week + daily stories
- **Content Types:**
  - Carousels: 2x per week
  - Reels/Videos: 2-3x per week
  - Single Image Posts: 2x per week
  - LinkedIn Articles: 1x per week
  - Stories: Daily (behind-the-scenes, polls, Q&A)

### Monthly Themes:
- **Month 1:** SEEDS Method Foundation (Awareness & Education)
- **Month 2:** Deep Dive into Sales Challenges (Engagement & Trust Building)
- **Month 3:** Transformation Stories & Offers (Conversion & Booking Calls)

### Best Posting Times:
- **LinkedIn:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 12-1 PM EST
- **Instagram:** Monday-Friday, 9-11 AM or 6-8 PM EST

## Success Metrics & Adjustments

### Track Weekly:
- **Engagement Rate:** Likes + Comments + Saves / Followers (Target: 3-5%)
- **Saves:** High saves = valuable content (Target: 20+ per carousel)
- **Profile Visits:** Traffic driven to profile (Target: 100+ per week)
- **Website Clicks:** Link clicks in bio (Target: 50+ per week)
- **DM Inquiries:** Direct messages about services (Target: 10+ per week)

### Monthly Review:
- Identify top 3 performing posts
- Identify bottom 3 performing posts
- Adjust content mix based on data
- Survey audience for content preferences

### Quarterly Adjustments:
- Refresh underperforming content types
- Double down on high performers
- Test new formats
- Update CTAs based on business goals
