## Bonus Tips for Maximum Engagement:

1. **Use visual hooks:** Hold up props, write on a whiteboard, use hand gestures
2. **Keep it short:** 30-60 seconds for maximum virality (except story videos can go 60-90 seconds)
3. **Use captions:** Most people watch without sound
4. **End with a question:** Drives comments and engagement
5. **Post consistently:** 3-5x per week for algorithm favor
6. **Use trending audio:** When it fits naturally
7. **Be authentic:** Your personality is your differentiator
8. **Test and iterate:** Track what resonates and double down
