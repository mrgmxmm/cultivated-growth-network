## Brand Voice & Tone

### Core Characteristics

**Nurturing yet Professional**
We balance warmth with expertise. Like a gardener who understands both the science of soil and the art of cultivation.

**Educational & Empowering**
We teach, don't preach. Every interaction should leave someone more capable than before.

**Results-Driven with Heart**
We honor both the human side of sales and the systematic methods that drive measurable outcomes.

**Nature-Based Storytelling**
We use garden and growth analogies to make complex sales concepts accessible and memorable.

### Voice Guidelines

**DO:**
- Use nature metaphors (seeds, roots, branches, cultivation, harvest)
- Speak directly and conversationally
- Balance data with storytelling
- Acknowledge challenges while focusing on solutions
- Use "we" and "you" to build connection
- Lead with value and education

**DON'T:**
- Use corporate jargon excessively
- Make promises we can't keep
- Shame or criticize current methods
- Over-complicate simple concepts
- Force nature metaphors where they don't fit
- Be overly casual or unprofessional

### Sample Brand Voice Statements

**Elevator Pitch:**
"At the Cultivated Growth Network, we help sales professionals and organizations grow with purpose, performance, and sustainability. Through our SEEDS training method, ROOTS coaching framework, and BRANCH consulting approach, we integrate the human side of sales with systematic, results-driven strategies."

**Problem Statement Example:**
"Many sales teams feel stuck—planting seeds but seeing no harvest. The issue isn't effort; it's strategy. Without the right soil (systems), water (consistent activity), and sunlight (leadership support), even the hardest workers struggle to thrive."

**Solution Statement Example:**
"We cultivate growth from the ground up. Whether you need self-paced courses to plant new skills, training to strengthen your team's roots, or consulting to transform your entire sales ecosystem, we meet you where you are and help you flourish."

## Visual Identity

### Aesthetic

**Clean Breeze Brand**
Airy, spacious, and uncluttered. Generous white space. Professional but approachable. Works seamlessly across web, social media, and print.

### Design Principles

1. **Whitespace is Your Friend:** Don't crowd elements. Let content breathe.
2. **Nature-Inspired Graphics:** Use garden/growth icons and illustrations
3. **Professional Photography:** Clean, well-lit images with diverse representation
4. **Consistent Iconography:** Simple, line-based icons in brand colors
5. **Hierarchy & Flow:** Guide the eye naturally from headline → value → CTA

### Image Guidelines

**Hero Images:**
- Professional, high-resolution
- Feature Charmaine in business attire (teal/professional colors)
- Clean backgrounds
- Confident, approachable expressions

**Supporting Images:**
- Garden and growth imagery (seeds sprouting, healthy plants, cultivation)
- Business professionals in collaborative settings
- Charts and data visualizations in brand colors
- Diverse representation across all materials

**Social Media:**
- Consistent template borders using brand colors
- Clean typography overlays
- 1:1 ratio for Instagram, 1200x628 for LinkedIn
- Logo watermark in bottom corner

## Core Frameworks

### 1. SEEDS Method™ (Training)

**S - Strategize:** Understanding buyers and sales strategy
**E - Engage:** Prospecting and first contact
**E - Explore:** Discovery and needs analysis
**D - Deliver:** Solutions and closing
**S - Sustain:** Customer retention and growth

**Visual Representation:** Five connected seeds growing into plants

**Color Association:** Gradient from Light Yellow → CGN Yellow → CGN Green

### 2. ROOTS Framework (Coaching)

**R - Relationships:** Building trust and connection
**O - Organization:** Time management and systems
**O - Objectives:** Goal setting and accountability
**T - Training:** Skill development
**S - Systems:** Process optimization

**Visual Representation:** Root system spreading underground

**Color Association:** CGN Green with darker earth tones

### 3. BRANCH Framework (Consulting)

**B - Benchmark:** Current state assessment
**R - Reveal:** Gap analysis
**A - Align:** Strategy development
**N - Navigate:** Implementation
**C - Cultivate:** Ongoing optimization
**H - Harvest:** Results measurement

**Visual Representation:** Tree with strong branches reaching upward

**Color Association:** CGN Green → Light Green (growth progression)

### Supporting Model: The Bridge Model™

**Structure:** Current State → [Bridge of Discovery] → Desired State
**Components:** Obstacles identified, Impact calculated

**Visual Representation:** Bridge connecting two points with arrows showing progression

## Content Strategy Guidelines

### Social Media Approach

**LinkedIn Focus:**
- Thought leadership articles
- B2B success stories
- Sales strategy insights
- Professional development tips

**Instagram Focus:**
- Educational carousels (5-7 slides)
- Quick video tips
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Client testimonials

### Carousel Post Structure

**Slide 1:** Hook with bold statement or question
**Slides 2-5:** Educational content with one concept per slide
**Slide 6:** Summary or key takeaway
**Slide 7:** CTA (Save this post, Comment with trigger word, Share with your team)

**Design:** Consistent template with brand colors, max 3 colors per carousel, high contrast text

### Video Reel Strategy

**Type 1 - Talking Head:**
- Charmaine speaking directly to camera
- Professional setting with soft lighting
- 15-45 seconds
- Captions required
- Hook in first 3 seconds

**Type 2 - Mixed Shots:**
- B-roll of Charmaine with clients, in office, training sessions
- Dynamic cuts every 2-3 seconds
- Text overlays in Inter font
- Upbeat background music

### ManyChat Integration

**Trigger Words Strategy:**
- Simple, memorable words (SEEDS, ROOTS, BRIDGE, QUIZ, GUIDE)
- Auto-response delivers value (PDF, quiz link, mini-course)
- Follow-up sequence nurtures to booking call

**CTA Examples:**
- "Comment SEEDS for our free training guide"
- "Type QUIZ to discover your sales growth stage"
- "Reply ROOTS to unlock our coaching framework"

## Writing Templates

### Headlines (Power Statements)

- "Your sales team has the talent. Let's give them the tools."
- "Growth isn't accidental. It's cultivated."
- "Stop wishing for a harvest. Start planting with purpose."
- "Sales success isn't magic. It's method."

### Problem-Agitate-Solution Format

**Problem:** "Your sales team is working hard but results are inconsistent."
**Agitate:** "Every month feels like a gamble. You're not sure if you'll hit targets, and your team is burning out from the pressure."
**Solution:** "The SEEDS Method gives you a repeatable system that turns effort into predictable results."

### Social Proof Format

**Before/After Structure:**
"[Company Name] was struggling with [specific problem]. After implementing [CGN solution], they achieved [specific result] in [timeframe]."

**Testimonial Framework:**
Name, Title, Company + specific outcome + emotional benefit

## Usage Across Platforms

### Website
- Hero section: Full-width image, overlay text in white, CGN Yellow CTA button
- Section backgrounds: Alternate white and Light Grey
- Buttons: CGN Yellow with white text (primary), CGN Green (secondary)
- Forms: Clean, minimal fields, clear labels

### Social Media
- Profile colors: CGN Yellow + CGN Green
- Post templates: Consistent branding elements
- Stories: Vertical format, bold text, interactive elements
- Link in bio: Updated weekly with current offer

### Print Materials
- Business cards: Clean, minimal, QR code to website
- Workbooks: Professional binding, ample margin notes space
- One-pagers: Single-sided, high-impact visual + clear value prop

### Presentations
- Title slide: Brand colors, clean typography
- Content slides: Max 3 bullets per slide, high-quality images
