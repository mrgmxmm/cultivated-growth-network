## Usage Guidelines

### Posting Frequency
- 2 carousels per week (LinkedIn + Instagram)
- Post on Tuesday and Thursday for max engagement
- Repost high-performing carousels after 60-90 days

### Engagement Strategy
1. **Respond to all comments within 2 hours** (especially ManyChat trigger words)
2. **Reply with personalized follow-up**, not just "check your DMs"
3. **Encourage saves and shares** (algorithm boost)
4. **Tag relevant accounts** when sharing (amplify reach)

### Analytics to Track
- **Saves** (most important for educational content)
- **Shares** (amplification indicator)
- **Comments with trigger words** (lead generation)
- **Profile visits from post** (awareness metric)
- **DM requests** (conversion metric)

### Design Consistency
- Always use brand colors and fonts
- Include CGN logo watermark
- Keep text concise and scannable
- Use high-contrast color combinations for readability
- Test carousel readability on mobile before posting
