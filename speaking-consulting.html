Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between consulting and training?

Training equips your team with skills and frameworks. Consulting includes strategy development, process design, hands-on implementation, and organizational transformation. Think of training as teaching someone to fish; consulting is helping them build a fishing business.

How long do consulting engagements typically last?

Project-based engagements typically run 3-6 months. Retained consulting can last 6-24+ months depending on scope. Fractional CSO roles are typically 12-18 month commitments.

Do you work with companies outside your local area?

Yes! We work with clients nationwide (and internationally). Many engagements can be delivered virtually, with strategic in-person sessions as needed. Travel expenses are separate from consulting fees.

Can we start with speaking and add consulting later?

Absolutely! Many consulting relationships start with a keynote or workshop where we identify opportunities for deeper engagement. There's no obligation to move to consulting after a speaking engagement.

What industries do you specialize in?

We've worked across B2B and B2C industries including manufacturing, professional services, SaaS, healthcare, financial services, and retail. Our frameworks are industry-agnostic but customized to your specific context.

Do you provide implementation support or just strategy?

We're hands-on. Unlike consultants who deliver a deck and leave, we roll up our sleeves and help you implement. You'll have ongoing access throughout the engagement for questions, troubleshooting, and adjustments.
