SEEDS™ Training Method

Five interconnected modules that create a complete, systematic approach to sales success

1

Strategize: Understanding Buyers

Half-Day Workshop | 4 Hours

Learn to create detailed buyer avatars and align your sales strategy with market realities. This foundational module ensures every team member understands who they're selling to and why.

What You'll Learn:

  • Four Pillars of Buyer Avatars: Demographics, Psychographics, Motivations, and Buying Triggers
  • Difference between B2B and B2C buyer research
  • How to gather buyer intelligence ethically and efficiently
  • Aligning sales strategy with buyer needs

Deliverables:

  • Pre-training diagnostic quiz
  • Buyer Avatar Worksheet
  • Research strategy template
  • Sales strategy alignment guide
2

Engage: The Art of Prospecting

Half-Day Workshop | 4 Hours

Master the 5x5 Prospecting Rule and build a multi-channel prospecting system that fills your pipeline consistently. Learn to build trust from first contact.

What You'll Learn:

  • The 5x5 Prospecting Rule: 5 touches × 5 days = 25 weekly touches
  • Trust Builders vs. Trust Killers framework
  • Multi-channel prospecting sequences (phone, email, social, direct mail)
  • Prospecting mindset: Consistency, Resilience, Process Focus

Deliverables:

  • 5x5 Prospecting Planner
  • Multi-channel sequence templates
  • Trust Builders checklist
  • Prospecting scripts and examples
3

Explore: Discovery That Wins Deals

Half-Day Workshop | 4 Hours

Learn The Bridge Model™ for uncovering true customer needs. Ask questions that lead to natural closes and position your solution as the logical next step.

What You'll Learn:

  • The Bridge Model™: Current State → Desired State
  • Identifying obstacles and calculating impact
  • Effective questioning techniques (open, probing, confirming)
  • Active listening and note-taking strategies

Deliverables:

  • Bridge Model™ template
  • Discovery question bank
  • Active listening checklist
  • Discovery call framework
4

Deliver: Solutions & Closing

Half-Day Workshop | 4 Hours

Present solutions that resonate and handle objections with confidence. Learn closing techniques that feel consultative, not pushy.

What You'll Learn:

  • Solution positioning based on discovery insights
  • Objection handling framework
  • Trial closes and buying signals
  • Closing techniques for different scenarios

Deliverables:

  • Solution presentation template
  • Objection response guide
  • Closing techniques playbook
  • Proposal frameworks
5

Sustain: Customer Retention & Growth

Half-Day Workshop | 4 Hours

Transform one-time buyers into lifelong customers. Build referral systems and strategies for growing account value over time.

What You'll Learn:

  • Customer onboarding and success strategies
  • Relationship management systems
  • Upselling and cross-selling approaches
  • Creating referral programs that work

Deliverables:

  • Customer success playbook
  • Account growth strategy template
  • Referral request scripts
  • Retention metrics dashboard

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need all 5 SEEDS™ modules or can I pick individual ones?

You can purchase individual modules based on your team's specific needs. However, the SEEDS™ Method is designed as an integrated system, so we recommend completing all five modules for maximum impact. We can help you prioritize which modules to start with.

How long does each training module take?

Each core SEEDS™ module is designed as a half-day (4-hour) workshop. This can be delivered as a single session or split into shorter segments over multiple days based on your team's schedule.

What's the difference between training and coaching?

Training is group-based, curriculum-driven learning focused on skills and processes. Coaching is personalized, one-on-one (or small group) guidance focused on individual performance, mindset, and accountability. Many organizations benefit from combining both.

Can you customize the training for our industry?

Absolutely! While the core frameworks are universal, we customize examples, case studies, role-plays, and exercises to reflect your specific industry, products, and sales challenges.

What's included in the 90-day implementation support?

After training, we provide check-in calls, email support, access to additional resources, and help troubleshooting challenges as your team implements what they've learned. This ensures the training sticks and delivers real results.

Do you offer train-the-trainer programs?

Yes! If you have internal trainers or managers who want to deliver the SEEDS™ Method to their teams, we offer train-the-trainer certification programs that include facilitator guides, slides, and ongoing support.
