# Video Reel Scripts for CGN

**Technical Specs:**
- Duration: 15-45 seconds
- Format: Vertical (9:16) for Instagram/TikTok, Square (1:1) for LinkedIn
- Always include captions (80% watch without sound)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Clear CTA in last 3 seconds
- Include upbeat background music (low volume, don't overpower voice)

**Brand Guidelines:**
- Professional but approachable
- Use nature/growth analogies where appropriate
- Speak directly to pain points
- Educational over promotional
- Include CGN logo watermark

## Video Production Notes

### Equipment Recommendations:
- **Camera:** iPhone 13+ or professional camera
- **Lighting:** Ring light or softbox lighting (avoid harsh shadows)
- **Microphone:** Lavalier mic or external mic (clear audio is critical)
- **Editing:** CapCut, Adobe Premiere, or InShot

### Filming Best Practices:
1. **Lighting:** Always light from front, avoid backlighting
2. **Background:** Clean, professional, not distracting
3. **Framing:** Rule of thirds, leave space at top of frame
4. **Audio:** Record in quiet space, use external mic
5. **Multiple Takes:** Film 3-5 takes of each reel for options

### Posting Strategy:
- **Instagram Reels:** Post 3-4x per week
- **LinkedIn Videos:** Post 2x per week (repurpose reels)
- **Best Times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM or 6-8 PM
- **Always Include:** Captions, hashtags, clear CTA
- **Pin Top Performer:** Pin best-performing reel to profile

### Engagement Tactics:
1. Respond to all comments within 2 hours
2. Use question stickers in stories to drive engagement
3. Repost user-generated content
4. Create "Part 2" reels for high performers
5. Test different hooks‚Äîswap if not working
